A Note On GPS Accuracy

The accuracy of the GPS in your device will improve over time after it has been started. You may wish to update the location information in your observation or census periodically if the current accuracy is insufficient. This can be done by pressing the "{{UPDATE_BUTTON_NAME}}" button.
